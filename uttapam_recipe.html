Ingredients

  • 1 cup leftover cooked rice
  • 1/2 cup leftover cooked vegetables (such as onions, tomatoes, peppers, or carrots)
  • 1/4 cup leftover cooked legumes (such as lentils, beans, or peas)
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 1 tablespoon chopped cilantro(coriander)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the cooked rice, cooked vegetables, cooked legumes, ginger, cilantro, cumin seeds, turmeric powder, garam masala, salt, and pepper.
  3. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  4. Drop the rice mixture by the spoonful onto the skillet and spread it out into a thin pancake shape.
  5. Cook for 2-3 minutes per side, or until golden brown and crispy.
  6. Transfer the uttapams to a baking sheet.
  7. Top each uttapam with your favorite pizza toppings, such as pizza sauce, cheese, pepperoni, mushrooms, or olives.
  8. Bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Then serve it hot and healthy.
